About Me

I’m Jason Shull, a game developer and programmer with a love for bringing ideas to life through code. I’ve loved video games for as long as I can remember—both playing them and creating them. As a Game Designer, I’m always on the lookout for new titles that spark ideas and fuel my creativity. Whether it’s an indie gem or a AAA blockbuster, every game has something to teach, and I’m here to learn, play, and build something awesome. Beyond design, I'm also committed to growing as a developer. I'm continually learning new coding principles and expanding my technical toolkit to bring my game ideas to life with precision and flair. I also love watching anime too which sometimes I get soo entranced in some of the art in the show that I try to make it. Either as 3d models or sketches thought most of them I would say arent in a well drawn/modeled manner.
