    ghosts-n-games is a game... as a programmer i did X Have you ever experienced a creepy glitch while trying to enjoy a session of Grab that Auto? Found a cartridge loaded into your console that you never touched in ages? Seen a beloved video game character wink at you in a way that seemed... demonic? Call Father Solomon now, to exorcise whatever malevolent spirits may be haunting your video gaming equipment and hardware! A professional esports player, longtime member of the Catholic Church, rehabilitated basement dweller, and experienced exorcist who can handle any gaming problem from beyond our mortal plane! Games n' Ghosts is a short Warioware-inspired game involving hopping between video games while emulating the power of Christ himself to cast out evil spirits. Where does one find where these spirits manifest? Why, whenever a game even so much as mentions a ghost, it gives a spirit so much as a chance to appear, too. Find the "ghosts", to find your spirit in need of exorcizing.
